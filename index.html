Ethan Brookins

1st AC First Assistant Camera / Focus Puller

Ethan is a Los Angeles-based 1st AC who loathes writing anything self-promotional, but loves putting cameras and lenses together. The intersection of technical, mechanical, and artistic problem-solving is one of his favorite aspects of filmmaking. It is the core of how he collaborates with cinematographers to create interesting and impactful images. His goal is to continually expand his knowledge, refine his workflow, and improve his craft.
